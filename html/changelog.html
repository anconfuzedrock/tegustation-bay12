20 January 2022

EgorDinamit updated:

  • Vox traders(NPC) now always want to purchase human mobs. Living players cost more.
  • Appraise verb, available to players with Finance skills, now can be used on almost anything around you. You can finally tell your coworkers how much they'd cost on the black market, yay!

19 January 2022

EgorDinamit updated:

  • Players will now spawn in a random cryopod, instead of always spawning from one in the corner. Sleep time on start isn't random anymore.
  • Using AI law modules in hand now shows their stored lawset.
  • Traitor gamemode can start with 0 players and 0 antagonists on round-start.
  • Revolution gamemode no requires at least 10 players to start.

EgorDinamit, SierraKomodo (BayStation 12) updated:

  • Standardized health for structures, turfs, and items. In short - less shitcode.
  • Closets can now be broken open with any item that has more than 20 force(damage).

15 January 2022

EgorDinamit updated:

  • Removed "stripper" clothes from the code.

13 January 2022

Doctor-Derp updated:

  • Updates the AI and Upload to look better and function better.

11 January 2022

EgorDinamit updated:

  • Electrical lockers spawn with 2 insulated gauntlets now, for Vox.
  • Engineering storage on Torch now contains 2 magnetic claws, for Vox.

Shadowtail117 updated:

  • Wasgaelhi is now less potent as an alcohol.
  • Mumbak sting will now intoxicate people similar to alcohol.
  • Toggling (or trying to toggle) regenerative auras provides more immersive messages.
  • The "Crew Transfer Factor" that determines the weight of transfer votes has been renamed to a more eponymous "Transfer Vote Multiplier".

10 January 2022

EgorDinamit updated:

  • Vox species can now work on SEV Torch. Their positions are heavily limited to contractor ranks only. They can also be a merchant now.
  • Added 2 Vox clothes options to loadouts.
  • Vox spawn with sandals on now.
  • Simple mobs no longer take ~2 seconds between the death message and actual death.
  • Simple mobs will now properly die when fighting each other without a living player in sight.
  • Ranged hivebots now have a fire delay and an increased projectile dispersion.
  • Ghosts following an object/mob will now orbit it.
  • Ghosts will now float. Yay.

05 January 2022

EgorDinamit updated:

  • Ghosts now have 3 buttons on the HUD: "Orbit", "re-enter corpse" and "teleport".
  • Each new shift may have different station(ship) traits. Read command report at round-start to see what you got.

KareTa, _Elar_ and few adjustments by EgorDinamit updated:

  • AI hud.

31 December 2021

EgorDinamit updated:

  • 2 new posters added.
  • A lot of Bay posters were deleted, mainly low-quality ones.
  • Buttons now provide a message saying "Access denied" if you try to interact with them while lacking proper access level.
  • RND Console will properly notify you of your insufficient access.
  • A lot of old browsers UIs are replaced with better-looking browser popups.
  • Admins can send custom text blurbs to players.

28 December 2021

EgorDinamit updated:

  • Containment fields now shock mobs and structures only when they move into them, instead of randomly doing that when in close proximity.
  • Bicaridine, Imidazoline, and Alkysine are now available in sleepers on various stages of stock parts upgrade.
  • Hyronalin is now available at the first upgrade tier of sleepers. Kelotane has been moved to tier 2.
  • If power goes out - occupants will automatically leave sleepers.
  • There will be a location blurb displayed when you join the round.

EgorDinamit, Kor (/tg/) updated:

  • Christmas (gift) tree will appear in the place of a fountain on deck 3 from December 1st to January 13th.
  • Santa's bag can hold fewer items, but now it can contain items of nearly any size and spawns gifts every 30 seconds while worn.

Shadowtail117 updated:

  • Unathi can now wear command HCMs. Rejoice.

21 December 2021

EgorDinamit updated:

  • To go into cyborg recharge station now you have to mouse-drop yourself OR another mob into it.
  • Cyborg recharge station will force its occupant to go out if power goes out. Same happens if you try to enter it without power.
  • Meraline reagent is added, a chemical effective at healing brute damage. The recipe is Bicaridine, Invaprovaline, and Iron.
  • Rechargers can now be upgraded with better capacitors to increase the rate at which items are being recharged.

UDaV73rus updated:

  • Singularity sprites have been updated.

17 December 2021

Shadowtail117 updated:

  • Using a stethoscope is now formatted correctly.
  • Unathi hearts now sound like two individual pulses.
  • Under 280 degrees Kelvin (about 6 degrees Celsius), an unathi's regenerative ability will shut down. Get warmer to turn it back on.

16 December 2021

EgorDinamit updated:

  • Pointing at an object is a bit more precise now. The arrow sprite itself has also been adjusted and made a bit bigger.
  • Explosions will now cause fuel tanks to explode as well.

Shadowtail117 updated:
